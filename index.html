About Me

I'm Abhishek Kumar, a passionate freelance video editor based in Delhi, India. With over 4 years of experience, I specialize in creating engaging short-form and long-form content that captures attention and drives results.

My journey began with a background in mechanical engineering, but my creative passion led me to the world of video editing. I combine technical precision with artistic vision to deliver content that not only looks great but also serves its purpose effectively.

I work with clients globally, helping them transform their raw footage into compelling visual stories. From Instagram reels to YouTube videos, from advertisements to cinematic content, I bring ideas to life with creativity and professionalism.
